Fitzy: Fitness Made Easy

ABOUT US

”Fitzy” is a Health and wellness-based Web application. Users can work on their health by performing exercises to get fit, follow a diet by encouraging healthy eating habits, improve focus by meditating, track their daily activities. Users just need to choose a level of complexity and a set of exercises they prefer. 3D models, videos, or just detailed descriptions explain how to perform each workout.
